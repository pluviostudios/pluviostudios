Our Philosophy

WE ARE
ARTISTS
FIRST.

At Pluviostudios, every pixel has intention. We're a close-knit crew of developers, artists, writers, and dreamers who came together because we believe games can be more — more emotional, more original, more alive.

Our indie roots keep us agile, experimental, and always honest. We don't chase trends — we set them.

✦ Artist-led 🎨 Handcrafted Art 🧠 Story-first Design 🌐 Cross-platform 🔥 Passion-driven ♻️ Community-built
What Drives Us

THE
PLUVIO
WAY

01 — CRAFT

ART OVER ALGORITHM

We reject the factory model of game dev. Every mechanic, every frame, every sound is deliberate. We make things that could only come from humans who care deeply.

02 — COMMUNITY

BUILT WITH OUR PLAYERS

Our community is our co-dev team. We share prototypes, collect feedback, and build in public — because the best games are built in conversation.

03 — COURAGE

WEIRD IS WELCOME

If an idea is too strange, too risky, too original — that's a green flag. We make the games nobody else dares to, because those are the ones people never forget.
